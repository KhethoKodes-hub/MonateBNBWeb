Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind Monate BnB

Welcome to Monate BnB

Established in 2020 by historically disadvantaged individuals, Monate BnB is a proudly Black Woman Owned business that forms part of the Move Us n Shake Us group. Our name "Monate" means "pleasant" or "enjoyable" in Setswana, which perfectly captures our mission to provide delightful hospitality experiences.

Located in the heart of Kuruman, we've built our reputation on providing exceptional accommodation services while maintaining the high standards of quality that define all Move Us n Shake Us enterprises.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a welcoming space where travelers could experience authentic Northern Cape hospitality while enjoying modern comforts. Over the years, we've grown from a small guesthouse to a respected accommodation provider, thanks to our commitment to excellence and our deep roots in the community.

Our Connection to Move Us n Shake Us

As part of the broader Move Us n Shake Us group, we benefit from extensive experience in various fields including construction, civil engineering, and infrastructure development. This unique positioning allows us to maintain our properties to the highest standards and continuously improve our facilities.

Our management team brings wide expertise from various sectors, ensuring that Monate BnB operates with professional excellence while maintaining the warm, personal touch that our guests love.

We're proud to contribute to Africa's infrastructure development through our parent company while creating sustainable tourism experiences that benefit our local community.
