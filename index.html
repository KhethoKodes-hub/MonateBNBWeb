About Monate BnB

Welcome to Our Hospitality

Monate BnB, established in 2020, is a proudly Black Woman Owned business located in the heart of Kuruman. Our name "Monate" means "pleasant" or "enjoyable" in Setswana, which reflects our commitment to providing exceptional hospitality experiences.

As part of the broader Move Us n Shake Us group, we bring the same dedication to quality and service excellence to our accommodation offerings. Our team is passionate about creating memorable stays for our guests while contributing positively to our community.

We believe in sustainable tourism and regularly contribute to local charities and community development projects, ensuring that our business makes a meaningful impact beyond just providing comfortable accommodation.
