Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your check-in and check-out times? +

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is by 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Do you offer long-term stay discounts? +

Yes, we offer special rates for extended stays. Please contact us directly for long-term booking inquiries and we'll be happy to provide a customized quote.

What construction services do you provide? +

We offer a wide range of construction services including general building contracting, renovations, road works, civil engineering projects, and specialized services like dust suppression and invasive species clearing.

Are you available for emergency construction services? +

Yes, we provide emergency response services for urgent construction and maintenance needs. Contact us directly for immediate assistance.

Do you provide purified water services to businesses? +

Absolutely! We supply bottled water, water refill stations, ice cubes, juices, and carbonated drinks to businesses of all sizes. Contact us for bulk order pricing.
